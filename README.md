## What is YALA?

YALA is a 5-days 4-nights leadership and personal development workshop conducted by Scholastic Trust Singapore for top students from Primary 4 to Junior College 2 (Grades 4 to 12). YALA is specially designed to empower these scholars to reach their highest life goals, including enabling them to gain admission to top schools and universities.
